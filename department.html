Welcome To The KeMU Computer Science Department

About The Department

The KeMU Computer Science Department is among the core departments in the University in providing students with an enriching knowledge and foundation in Computer Science with application of global technologies to ensure that they are well prepared to compete successfully for careers or graduate studies. The department offers the students with an academic experience that develops core computer science knowledge and skills throughout its curriculum with a student centered approach to teaching and learning. Our faculty-supervised research is geared towards increasing participation of all students from diverse groups in the country and also world-wide. Our innovative teaching approaches tergeting core courses prepares our students for ever-evolving technological advancement.
